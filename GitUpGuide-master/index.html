Braille Kindle

I am currently working on an ITSP project called Braille Kindle. The idea is to make a refreshable kindle that can show numerous rows of Braille that can be felt by the reader. The current market solution is the Canute 300, which works on Piezo-electric model where a ceramic dot is excited by passing electricity. However this causes the Kindles to be very expensive making it unreachable to the under-privileged. The mechanism we propose is an electromagnetic one based on Solenoids.
